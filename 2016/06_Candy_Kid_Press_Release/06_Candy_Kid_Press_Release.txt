<b>Company Bio</b>
<br />
<b><i><a href="http://steveproxna.blogspot.com">StevePro Studios</a></i></b> is an independent game developer that builds and publishes 80s retro arcade video games!  The studio was founded by <i>Steven Boland</i> "<a href="http://www.twitter.com/SteveProXNA">SteveProXNA</a>" in January 2007 as a "one man team of one" and is currently based in Dublin, Ireland.
<br /><br />
<b>Fact Sheet</b>
<br />
The original 8-bit title <b><a href="http://www.segaretro.org/Candy_Kid">Candy Kid</a></b> was a "type-in" video game published by New Zealand's <i>Sega Computer <a href="http://www.smspower.org/Scans/SegaComputer-Magazine-Issue02?gallerypage=17">Magazine</a></i> in the September 1984 issue, pages 17-18.  Inspired by the <a href="http://steveproxna.blogspot.co.uk/2013/06/sega-retro-gaming.html">Sega Retro Gaming</a> scene, "<i>Candy Kid</i>" was re-written as "<i>Retro Candy Kid</i>" by <i>StevePro Studios</i> in September 2015.
